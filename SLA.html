5. Data Protection and Backup Services

5.1 UK Data Protection Compliance

Swift Peak Hosting is committed to compliance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018. We are registered with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO). We process personal data in accordance with our Data Processing Agreement (DPA), which forms an integral part of this service agreement.

Data Protection Measures:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols
  • Regular security assessments and penetration testing
  • Multi-factor authentication and access controls
  • Incident response procedures compliant with ICO guidelines
  • Staff training on UK data protection requirements
  • Secure data centres within the UK and EEA

5.2 Backup Services

5.2.1 Game Hosting Services

We perform automated backups for disaster recovery purposes. Customers can create, manage, and restore backups through the Game Panel. Backup retention periods vary by service plan (minimum 7 days for standard plans, 30 days for premium plans).

5.2.2 Other Hosting Services

Customers are responsible for creating and maintaining their own data backups. Optional managed backup services are available at additional cost. We provide backup tools and guidance but cannot guarantee data recovery in all circumstances.

Important: Our backups are for disaster recovery only and should not be considered a substitute for your own data backup strategy. We strongly recommend maintaining independent backups of critical data.

6. Security and DDoS Protection

6.1 Infrastructure Security

Physical Security:

  • Tier 3+ data centres with 24/7 monitoring
  • Biometric and keycard access controls
  • Redundant power supply (UPS and generators)
  • Advanced fire suppression systems
  • CCTV surveillance and security personnel

Network Security:

  • Multi-layer firewall protection
  • Intrusion detection and prevention systems
  • Network segmentation and VLANs
  • Regular security patches and updates
  • SSL/TLS certificate management

6.2 DDoS Protection Services

Protection Capabilities:

  • Layer 3/4 Protection: Volumetric and protocol attacks up to 10 Gbps
  • Layer 7 Protection: Application-layer attack mitigation
  • Always-On Protection: Continuous monitoring and automatic mitigation
  • Custom Rules: Configurable firewall and rate limiting
  • Real-time Monitoring: 24/7 SOC monitoring and response

Service Limitations:

While our DDoS protection provides robust defense against most common attacks, exceptionally large-scale attacks (>10 Gbps) may require additional mitigation services or temporary upstream filtering. We cannot guarantee protection against all types of sophisticated attacks.

7. Hardware and Infrastructure Management

7.1 Hardware Replacement Commitment

In the event of hardware failure, we commit to the following response times:

Component Type Target Replacement Time Service Impact
Critical Components (CPU, Motherboard) 24-48 hours Service unavailable
Storage Components (HDD/SSD) 12-24 hours Potential data access issues
Memory (RAM) 4-12 hours Performance degradation
Network Components 2-8 hours Connectivity issues

These are target times based on parts availability and data centre access. Actual replacement times may vary during weekends, bank holidays, or due to supply chain issues beyond our control.

7.2 Proactive Monitoring

Our infrastructure includes comprehensive monitoring systems that track hardware health, performance metrics, and potential issues. Our Network Operations Centre (NOC) provides 24/7 monitoring with proactive alerts to address potential problems before they impact service availability.
