  • Intrusion detection systems
  • Network segmentation
  • Regular security updates

6.2 DDoS Protection Services

Protection Capabilities:

  • Layer 3/4 Protection: Volumetric and protocol-based attacks up to 10 Gbps
  • Layer 7 Protection: Application-layer attack mitigation
  • Always-On Protection: Continuous monitoring and automatic mitigation
  • Custom Rules: Configurable firewall and rate limiting rules

While our DDoS protection provides robust defense against most common attacks, exceptionally large-scale attacks (>10 Gbps) may require additional mitigation services or temporary traffic routing adjustments.
