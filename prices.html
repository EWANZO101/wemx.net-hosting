Important Notice – WEMX Cloud Hosting

Please note that all WEMX Cloud Hosting installations can take from 2 to 12 hours to complete. Certain parts of the installation process cannot be fully automated.

If you have not received your WEMX installation within this time frame, you may open a support ticket.

We will send you an email within the 12-hour window containing a link that requires your action. For your safety, security, and to comply with GDPR, you will be asked to enter your WEMX domain and WEMX license key.

After submitting this information, please wait on the page. It will notify you when your WEMX store is live. If this does not happen, please open a ticket. We aim to respond within 2 to 12 hours.

Please read and understand this notice to avoid unnecessary tickets.
