Swift Peak Hosting - SnailyCAD Live Map Guide

Setup Instructions

Follow this guide to set up a live map for your FiveM server with SnailyCAD on Swift Peak Hosting.

Step 1: Configure a Public Hostname

  1. Navigate to the "Tunnels" section in your control panel.
  2. Set up a public hostname. Use the subdomain livemap and your domain (e.g., snailycad.co.uk).
  3. Set the service type to HTTP.
  4. Enter the URL pointing to your serverâ€™s IP and port (e.g., yourserverip:30121).
  5. Save the hostname configuration.

Step 2: Configure Live Map in SnailyCAD

  1. Log in to your SnailyCAD admin panel.
  2. Go to the "Integrations" section and click on "Live Map".
  3. Click "Add URL" and provide the following:
    • Name: livemap
    • URL: https://livemap.snailycad.co.uk
  4. Click "Add URL" and save your changes.

Step 3: Map Tiles Configuration

  1. Prepare your map tiles in the required format: minimap_sea_0_0.png, minimap_sea_0_1.png, etc.
  2. Ensure the files are in .png or .jpg format.
  3. Upload the tiles to SnailyCAD in the "Map Tiles" section under "Live Map Settings".
