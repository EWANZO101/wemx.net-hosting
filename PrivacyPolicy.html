9. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access (Article 15): Request copies of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification (Article 16): Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure (Article 17): Request deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances
  • Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18): Request limitation of processing in certain situations
  • Right to Data Portability (Article 20): Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Object (Article 21): Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: With your local supervisory authority
Important: To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at legal@swiftpeakhosting.com. We will respond within one month of receipt of your request.
