SnailyCAD Activation Timeline

All SnailyCAD orders typically take 2–12 hours to activate after payment confirmation. If you receive an error during this period, it is normal while your environment is being provisioned. You will receive a confirmation email once your instance is fully activated and ready to use.

  • Payment received: Activation window starts.
  • Provisioning environment (may show errors temporarily).
  • Confirmation email sent when activation completes.
  • Average time: 2–12 hours after payment.
