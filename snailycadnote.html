SnailyCAD Activation Process

Follow these simple steps to get your CAD system up and running

⚠️ Free Trial No Longer Available

The 14-day free trial for SnailyCAD has been discontinued.

We cannot update the official SnailyCAD documentation at this time, so please follow our updated activation process below.

1

Place Your Order

What happens: Complete your purchase through our secure checkout system.

Timeline: Your CAD will be automatically deployed within 2–12 hours after payment confirmation.

What you need to do: Simply complete the payment process - everything else is automated!

2

Provide Your Domain Information

What you need: A domain name for your CAD system.

Option 1 - Your Own Domain: Use a domain you already own (e.g., mycad.yourdomain.com)

Option 2 - Free Subdomain: Get a free subdomain from us (e.g., yourcad.swiftpeak.net)

Where to include this: Add your domain choice in the checkout notes section during purchase.

3

Cloudflare Users Only

If you use Cloudflare: We need additional information for proper setup.

Required: Include your Debian install code in the checkout notes.

This ensures your CAD works properly with Cloudflare's proxy settings.

Don't use Cloudflare? You can skip this step completely!

✓

Activation Complete!

What happens next: Our system automatically sets up your CAD.

You'll receive: An email notification when your CAD is ready to use.

Normal behavior: You may see temporary errors during the initial setup process - this is completely normal and will resolve automatically.

Ready to go: Once complete, you can start using your fully functional SnailyCAD system!
