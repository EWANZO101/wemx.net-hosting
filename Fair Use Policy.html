Fair Use Policy

At Swift Peak Hosting Ltd, we strive to provide reliable, high-performance hosting services for WEMX, SnailyCAD, VPS, and related solutions. To maintain a fair, secure, and efficient hosting environment for all our customers, we have established the following Fair Use Policy (FUP).

1. Purpose

This Fair Use Policy ensures equitable access to resources and maintains the quality and security of our services by preventing abuse, excessive use, or any activity that may degrade service for others.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all customers and users of Swift Peak Hosting Ltd services, including but not limited to WEMX hosting, SnailyCAD hosting, VPS servers, and related infrastructure.

3. Acceptable Use

Customers agree to use our services responsibly and ethically, including but not limited to:

  • Using resources within the allocated limits (CPU, RAM, bandwidth, storage).
  • Avoiding any activities that harm or disrupt Swift Peak Hosting Ltd services or other customers’ experience.
  • Complying with all applicable laws and regulations.
  • Respecting intellectual property rights and avoiding illegal content or activity.

4. Resource Usage Limits

To prevent service degradation, the following guidelines apply:

  • CPU and RAM Usage: Customers should keep usage within the agreed plan limits. Persistent overuse may lead to service throttling or suspension.
  • Bandwidth: Excessive or abusive bandwidth consumption (e.g., large-scale file sharing, DDoS attacks) is prohibited.
  • Storage: Users must stay within their allocated disk space and remove unnecessary files to avoid penalties.
  • Network Usage: Customers must not use the network for spamming, hacking, or any unauthorized access attempts.

5. Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited:

  • Hosting or distributing malware, viruses, or other harmful software.
  • Conducting unauthorized penetration tests or attacks on other systems.
  • Running illegal or pirated software or content.
  • Sending unsolicited mass emails or spam.
  • Using excessive server resources that impact other users’ services.
  • Any actions that violate Swift Peak Hosting Ltd’s Terms of Service or applicable laws.

6. Monitoring and Enforcement

Swift Peak Hosting Ltd monitors resource usage and activity to ensure compliance with this policy. If abuse or violations are detected, we may:

  • Contact the customer to address the issue.
  • Temporarily throttle or suspend the service.
  • Permanently terminate the service in cases of severe or repeated violations.

7. Customer Responsibilities

Customers are responsible for:

  • Understanding and managing their resource usage.
  • Reporting any security breaches or suspicious activities.
  • Maintaining backups of their data.
  • Keeping software and applications up to date and secure.

8. Changes to the Policy

Swift Peak Hosting Ltd reserves the right to update or modify this Fair Use Policy at any time. Customers will be notified of significant changes via email or through their account dashboard.
